# Short Executive Version

## EDDIE in One Paragraph

**EDDIE is an open, decentralised energy data space infrastructure** that enables secure, consent-based, and interoperable access to energy data across Europe and beyond. It is **not a commercial data marketplace**, but a **neutral foundation layer** designed to support a participative, sustainable, and digital energy system.
